## Project Overview

Undertook the comprehensive redesign and revamp of the official Petrosains website. The project's core objective was to create a more modern, user-friendly, and effective online platform to significantly enhance support for STEM advocacy and education initiatives across Malaysia.

*   Led the redesign effort to overhaul and refresh the entire website interface.
